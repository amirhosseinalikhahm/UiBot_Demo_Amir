What is Robotic Process Automation?

The word Robotic, in RPA, might sound ambiguous to many of us as when it comes to robots we usually think of some machine that is capable of mimicking the human actions. However, this time the robots are prepared to serve us in a different shape as a piece of software on our computer. These robots are capable of performing any tasks with three given characteristics below.

Repetitive

Rule-based

High Volume

Therefore, we can define RPA as a robot that can mimic human actions to do rule-based, repetitive tasks with high volume. These robots are based on software and Artificial Intelligence, which makes their performance desirable and accurate. RPA robots can perform a variety of tasks, for a better understanding of their functionality, please take a look at the examples given below.

Logging in into applications/accounts

Move, Copy & paste data, files and folders

Extract data from websites

Read & write data from databases

Process the data and making calculations

Extract data from Docs, PDFs, Emails and forms

Now that we have got a relatively clear picture of the potential of RPA robots, let's dig deeper into this and see how can we combine these capabilities and manipulate them in different areas in the education industry.

RPA robots & Education Industry!

This section is dedicated to describe the scenarios and use cases that RPA robots can automate the tasks with high accuracy and efficiency.

Application processing, Enrolment, Course registration & Student evaluation

  • RPA Robots are capable of checking the students' qualifications and evaluating them and shortlisting the candidates for the final check.
  • They are also capable of evaluating the enrolment documents and checking the factors such as GPA, prerequisites, and so on for the course selection. The list of courses that can be offered to each student can be compared to the list of courses they want and in the end, register them for the course and send them their timetable.

Attendance Management, Student Grading & Evaluation

  • RPA robots can use image processing & machine learning techniques for optical mark recognition, evaluating the examination documents/reports and generate the final reports and finally send the report to school and upload them on the website.
  • They are also able to track and manage the attendance of the students and inform the class instructor, school, and parents in case of absence using RFID cards.

Chatbots, Update and Arrangement of Meetings and Timetables

  • RPA robots also can manipulate the Natural Language Processing (NLP) and other machine learning tools to process the queries of the students, staff, applicants, and others and reply to them like a human.
  • The meetings and other events are also a big challenge for everyone to find a suitable time that everyone could attend and then decide on it and inform everyone to attend it. The robots can get the schedule of the attendees and then find a common empty slot on their schedule and finally, inform everyone via email and update their timetable.
